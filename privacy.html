Gritwise

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 20, 2026

Gritwise ("we", "our", or "us") is a mobile app that helps you plan off-road and overlanding trips using AI-powered route planning and offline maps. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it uses that information, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly. When you use Gritwise, you enter trip details such as destinations, travel dates, vehicle type, and route preferences. This information is stored locally on your device and is never uploaded to our servers.

Device location. Gritwise may request access to your device's location to center the map on your current position. Location is used only within the app and is not transmitted to us or stored beyond your current session.

Purchase information. If you subscribe to Gritwise Pro, payment is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not receive or store your payment card details.

2. Information Shared With Third-Party Services

To provide its features, Gritwise sends certain data to the following third-party services. We do not sell your data to any party.

Anthropic (Claude AI). When you generate or edit a trip plan, the trip details you have entered (destinations, dates, vehicle profile, preferences) are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate route suggestions. Anthropic's use of this data is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not include personally identifying information in these requests.

MapTiler. Gritwise uses MapTiler to display maps and to convert place names into geographic coordinates (geocoding). Place name queries are sent to MapTiler's servers for this purpose. MapTiler's use of this data is governed by MapTiler's Privacy Policy.

OpenRouteService. Geographic coordinates for your planned waypoints are sent to OpenRouteService to calculate drivable routes. OpenRouteService's use of this data is governed by OpenRouteService's Privacy Policy.

OpenStreetMap / Overpass API. Gritwise queries the public Overpass API (based on OpenStreetMap data) to find nearby points of interest such as campsites. No personal data is included in these queries.

3. Data Storage and Security

All trip data, vehicle profiles, and map tile caches are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data is not synced to any cloud service in this version of the app. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app.

We take reasonable technical measures to protect data transmitted to third-party services, including using HTTPS for all network requests.

4. Children's Privacy

Gritwise is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us at the address below.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
