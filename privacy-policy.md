# Gritwise Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** May 3, 2026  
**Last updated:** May 3, 2026

---

## Who we are

Gritwise is an off-road trip planning app. "We," "us," and "our" refer to the developer of the Gritwise application. Questions about this policy can be sent to **privacy@gritwise.app** (update this address before launch).

---

## What data Gritwise collects and why

### Data stored only on your device

The following data is created when you use Gritwise and is stored exclusively in a local database on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers.

| Data | Why we store it |
|------|----------------|
| Trip information (destination, dates, vehicle preferences, notes) | To save and display your planned trips |
| AI-generated route details (waypoints, day plans, campsites) | To let you view and edit your routes offline |
| Vehicle profile (nickname, type, ground clearance, 4WD capability) | To tailor route suggestions to your rig |
| A first-run flag indicating you have completed onboarding | To avoid showing the welcome screen on every launch |

Deleting the Gritwise app from your device permanently removes all of this data.

### Email address (optional, collected at save)

Gritwise does not ask for your email at launch. If you attempt to save a trip after reaching the free tier limit, you will be prompted to provide an email address to continue. This email is used solely to:

- Associate your free or Pro subscription status with your account
- Send transactional messages (receipt of payment, account changes) if you subscribe to Gritwise Pro

We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

## Third-party services

Gritwise relies on the following third-party services to function. When you use these features, your data is subject to those providers' own privacy policies as well as ours.

### Anthropic Claude API

When you describe a trip or ask for route suggestions, the text you enter is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a travel plan. Anthropic processes this data according to their [Privacy Policy](https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy). We do not send your name, email, or any identifier to Anthropic — only the trip description text.

### MapTiler

Gritwise displays maps powered by MapTiler. When you view the map, your device makes tile requests to MapTiler's servers, which may log your IP address as part of standard CDN operation. MapTiler's [Privacy Policy](https://www.maptiler.com/privacy-policy/) applies to these requests.

### OpenRouteService

When Gritwise calculates a driving route, the waypoint coordinates you have chosen are sent to OpenRouteService (operated by HeiGIT, Heidelberg Institute for Geoinformation Technology). No personal identifiers are included in these requests. OpenRouteService's [Terms of Service](https://openrouteservice.org/terms-of-services/) and privacy practices apply.

### OpenStreetMap / Overpass API

Gritwise fetches points of interest (campsites, fuel stops, etc.) from the OpenStreetMap Overpass API using a geographic bounding box. No personal data is included in these requests. OpenStreetMap's [Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy) applies.

## Data we do not collect

- **Precise location / GPS data.** Gritwise does not request location permission in its current version. You type your destination; we do not track where you are.
- **Device identifiers or advertising IDs.** Gritwise does not use advertising SDKs or collect device fingerprints.
- **Usage analytics or crash reporting.** The current version does not include an analytics SDK.
- **Payment data.** If you subscribe to Gritwise Pro, payment is processed through Apple App Store or Google Play. We never see or store your card number or billing details.

| Data type | Retention |
|-----------|-----------|
| Trip and vehicle data (on-device) | Until you delete the trip, uninstall the app, or erase your device |
| Email address | Until you request deletion (see below) |

## Your rights

You may request deletion of any data we hold about you at any time by emailing **privacy@gritwise.app**. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or California, you may also have the right to access, correct, or port your personal data. Contact us using the address above to exercise these rights.

## Children's privacy

Gritwise is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
